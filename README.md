# Statistical Intuition and Applications — Course Redesign (Summer 2025)

This repository documents the Summer 2025 redesign of the course *Statistical Intuition and Applications*. The redesign was developed to provide first-year undergraduate students with a structured overview of fundamental concepts in statistics and probability, with an emphasis on applications in business contexts.

The course was designed for undergraduate students enrolled in the Entrepreneurship and Innovation program.

As part of the Summer 2025 redesign, all breakout activities were developed from scratch and grounded in real-world business scenarios to support applied learning and classroom engagement.

## Repository Contents

The repository includes the following materials:

- **PDF files** containing step-by-step instructions for each session, organized into:
  - **Pre-Class Work (PCW)**
  - **Hook**
  - **Polls**
  - **Breakout Activities**
  - **Closure Activities**
- **CSV datasets** for classroom use and applied exercises
- **Jupyter Notebook templates** to support guided analysis and reproducible workflows
